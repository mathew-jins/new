                                                   BREAK THE CHAIN

COVID-19 is a disease caused by a new strain of coronavirus. ‘CO’stands for corona, ‘VI’ for virus, and‘ D’ for disease. Formerly, this disease was referred to as ‘2019 novel coronavirus’ or ‘2019-nCoV.’ The COVID-19 virus is a new virus linked to the same family of viruses as Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome [SARS] and some types of common cold.
                                                Symptoms
▶FEVER
▶ COUGH
▶Shortness of Breath
 * In more severe cases, infection can cause pneumonia or breathing difficulties.These symptoms are similar to the flu (influenza) or the common cold, which are a lot more common than COVID-19.
                              Steps to Break the Chain
Frequently wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds [When soap and running water are unavailable, use an alcohol-based hand rub with at least 60% alcohol]
Avoid touching your eyes, nose, or mouth with unwashed hands
Avoid close contact with people who are sick
Avoid exposure to public
Avoid unwanted gatherings
Avoid road trips and all sorts of unwanted travels
Stay home
stay safe and this will break the chain of covid
